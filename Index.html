3-Day Family-Friendly Tour of Donegal

Day 1: Southwest Donegal – Coastal Beauty & Heritage

  • Slieve League Cliffs: Spectacular cliff views. More info
  • Killybegs Harbour: Ireland's top fishing port.
  • Lunch: Local seafood restaurants in Killybegs.
  • Glencolmcille Folk Village: Traditional Irish village museum. More info
  • Evening: Return to Drumnasillagh for a relaxed evening.

Day 2: Central Donegal – Nature & Relaxation

  • Glenveagh National Park: Castle, gardens, and family-friendly trails. More info
  • Picnic: In one of the park's picnic areas.
  • Mount Errigal Viewpoint: Scenic mountain drive.
  • Dinner: Eat out in Letterkenny.

Day 3: Inishowen Peninsula – Scenic Drives & Historical Sites

  • Grianán of Aileach: Ancient stone fort with panoramic views. More info
  • Inishowen 100 Scenic Drive: Leisurely coastal drive. More info
  • Lunch: Stop in Buncrana for food with a view.
  • Evening: Return to Drumnasillagh to wind down.

Travel Tips:

  • Ensure sites are stroller and wheelchair accessible.
  • Plan for rest stops and bring snacks and water.
  • Dress in layers and bring rain gear due to unpredictable weather.
