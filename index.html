Donegal Family Tour – Day 1

Base: 1 Loughcrillan Rd, Glenties, Co. Donegal, F94 KT22

Slieve League Cliffs

Among Europe’s highest sea cliffs, offering stunning views and peaceful walking paths.

Distance: ~50 km | Drive Time: ~1 hour

Glencolmcille Folk Village

A living-history museum showcasing traditional Irish cottages and rural heritage from centuries past.

Distance: ~60 km | Drive Time: ~1 hr 15 min

Mount Errigal Viewpoint

Donegal’s tallest peak is a dramatic backdrop for photos. Short walks near the base offer fantastic views.

Letterkenny (Dinner)

Donegal’s largest town, filled with restaurants, shopping, and amenities. Perfect for an evening meal.

Day 3: Scenic History on the Inishowen Peninsula

Grianán of Aileach

An ancient ring fort with commanding views over Lough Swilly and the Inishowen peninsula. A peaceful and historic site.

Buncrana (Lunch)

A coastal town along Lough Swilly known for its beach, heritage trail, and family-friendly spots to eat and explore.
